About

  • 2004-2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Richard Elhaj Media (RE Media) is Egypt's premium content production and distribution organisation. RE Media manages and controls several brands in the area of music, video, online publishing, digital media, social media, online advertising, event organisation and TV production. The company was setup by Richard Elhaj a well known producer in the Arab World and has operated in the Middle East since 2004. The company controls a large portfolio of Audio & Video content and manages content and brands on behalf of many artists, companies & MNCs.

Services

Graphic Design & Branding

At RE Studios, we craft visual identities that do more than look good—they communicate who you are. Our team blends strategy, creativity, and storytelling to design brands that stand out in competitive markets.

Video Production

Video is the language of today’s world, and at RE Studios, we master every dialect of it. Our production team creates immersive visuals through expert editing, dynamic motion graphics, AI-enhanced scenes, and 2D & 3D animation.

Social Media Management

Managing social media is more than posting—it's strategy, consistency, and communication. We handle everything from content planning and scheduling to engagement and analytics.

Social Media Content

Great content doesn’t happen by chance—it’s crafted with intent. At RE Studios, we produce engaging and visually striking content tailored to each platform, from photography to promotional creatives.

Audio Production

Sound is a powerful storytelling tool. We create custom songs, brand jingles, and compelling voice-overs that add emotion, energy, and professionalism to your projects.

Audio Studios

Our audio studios are built for creators who demand excellence, providing the perfect space for recording, mixing, and mastering with state-of-the-art equipment and acoustic treatment.

Video Studio

Our video studio offers complete flexibility for any type of production, with premium lighting setups, backgrounds, and a modular environment for creative storytelling.
