About

Richard Elhaj Media

  • 2004-2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Richard Elhaj Media (RE Media) is Egypt's premium content production and distribution organisation. RE Media manages and controls several brands in the area of music, video, online publishing, digital media, social media, online advertising, event organisation and TV production. The company was setup by Richard Elhaj a well known producer in the Arab World and has operated in the Middle East since 2004. The company controls a large portfolio of Audio & Video content and manages content and brands on behalf of many artists, companies & MNCs.
